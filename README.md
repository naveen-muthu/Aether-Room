**# AETHER ROOM

## Project Title

**AETHER ROOM – A Digital Relaxation Environment for Mental Wellness and Focus**

---

## Problem Statement

In today's digital world, people experience stress, anxiety, and difficulty concentrating due to continuous work, studies, and excessive screen time. Existing entertainment platforms often increase distractions instead of helping users relax. Aether Room provides a peaceful digital environment where users can relax, improve focus, and enjoy immersive ambient experiences.

---

## Project Objective

The main objective of **Aether Room** is to create a virtual relaxation space where users can experience peaceful environments through immersive videos and ambient sounds.

### Objectives

- Provide immersive virtual relaxation rooms.
- Play ambient sounds such as rain, fireplace, storm, and lofi music.
- Help users reduce stress and improve concentration.
- Allow users to upload their own background videos and audio.
- Offer a distraction-free environment for studying, working, or relaxing.
- Enable a simple community chat for peaceful interaction.
- Support full-screen immersive mode for a better relaxation experience.
